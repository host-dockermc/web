About

What is DockerMC?

DockerMC is a hosting provider, created by BenVanDW as a way to provide private containerised and customisable Minecraft servers to people who are sick of complicated, confusing and clunky menus, simply log in, setup your server and go straight into modifying it exactly how you'd like

What does "containerised" mean

Normal Minecraft servers run on the bare hardware its just the common and simple process, with DockerMC we use a technology developed by Microsoft and the Open Source Communnity to package each server into its own virtual mini computer, by doing this your data stays in your container, you can customise as much as you'd like and no other person can threaten or alter your files.
